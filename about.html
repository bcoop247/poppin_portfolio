About

Hello, I'm Brandon! I am a dedicated and aspiring Software Engineer. My journey into the world of software development has been an exciting transition from my former employment role as a property and casualty claims adjuster. I am highly motivated to make a career shift and have always been intrigued by technology and its ever-evolving state. I love solving problems and find the scope of coding to be challenging, enjoyable, and highly rewarding. I have a solid understanding of the key fundamental principles of JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and the React framework. I have developed full-stack applications using React as the front end and express JS with PostgreSQL to run the back end.

I'm passionate about crafting clean, efficient code and thrive in collaborative environments where I can contribute my skills and absorb insights from experienced peers. I'm truly excited to combine my professional background with my newfound coding expertise to create impactful and user-centered software solutions that make a difference.

I studied Computer Science as a minor at Florida State University with a main focus on Criminology. I was unsure as to what I wanted to do in the long run until I began diving deeper into the world of development. I took a leap of faith and went through a 6-month Software Engineering Bootcamp at the State College of Florida. I found the boot camp to be highly enjoyable and am now focused on learning all aspects of developing software and web applications. In addition to coding, I also enjoy running, golfing, and going to the beach!
